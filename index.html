ofxBlud

a set of lua wrappers around a chunk of openframeworks

 

      		
    

    

      
        

          ¶
        

        
the core of blud more or less maps to the way that openframeworks is layed out
with the following additional components:



    

  •  bludGraphics a wrapper around some of the opengl parts of openframeworks. Useful for drawing rectangles.
    • 

  •  bludImage for drawing texture maps, or parts of texturemaps. Has some very basic sprite support.
    • 

  •  bludSynth a sample based synthesizer
    • 

  •  bludAudioSync an audio syncronized event system
    •
